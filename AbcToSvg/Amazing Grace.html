Symbols in Indonesian numerical notation (especially for Christian hymns)

(Sometimes the translation from Google is very confusing too. forgive if there is a lot of wrong understanding of music) 

Number:
Normal        : 0 1 2 3 4 5 6 7
Up 1 octav  : a1 a2 a3 a4 a5 a6 a7
Down 1 octav : s1 s2 s3 s4 s5 s6 s7
Up 2 oktav  : 91 92 93 94 95 96 97
Down 2 oktav : 81 82 83 84 85 86 87

j4k.5    :  4 = 1/2 knock; 5 = 1/4 knock.
j2k3.    :  2 = 1/2 knock; 3 and . = 1/4 knock.
J4k0k5l23:  4 = 1/2 knock; 0 and 5 = 1/4 knock; 2 and 3 = 1/8 knock.
k2L34    :  1 = 1/4 knock; 3 and 4 = 1/8 knock.
L3k2j14  :  3 and 2 = 1/8 knock;  1 = 1/4 knock; 4 = 1/2 knock.
