Define multicolumn printing.

command may be:

start
save the current vertical position and the left and right margins. Then, these margins may be changed to print music or text.
new
reset the vertical offset at the place of the previous %%multicol start, and restore the left and right margins.
end
restore the left and right margins, and skip down to a safe place.
